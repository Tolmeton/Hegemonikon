## 🚨 LATEST UPDATE (Paradigm Collapse & Pivot)
The "Cognitive Homeostasis" theory (which posited that maintaining low `t_gini` variance drives agent success) is **FALSE**. Phase B testing (N=500 Verified) revealed that the strong negative correlation with `t_gini` (BF10=507) was a statistical illusion caused by reverse causality: failed agents enter error loops at the end of their trajectories, causing massive `thought` spikes that artificially inflate the Gini coefficient post-hoc. 

A Forward Prediction Test using only the first half of the trajectories (`early_t_gini`) completely erased the correlation (N=500: r=0.10, BF10=0.7). The macroscopic Gini approach has failed. **We have officially accepted this Null Result.**
See `rom_2026-04-03_oblivion_paradigm_collapse.md` and `beb_oblivion_pivot_2026-04-03.md`.

- **[AXIOM]** 真の忘却メトリクスは、生のテキスト長や表面的な N-gram 一致ではなく、エージェントの「意味論的 (Semantic)」な圧縮または「行動空間 (Action Space)」の遷移確率を測定するものでなければならない。
